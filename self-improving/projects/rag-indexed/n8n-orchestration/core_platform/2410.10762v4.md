ABSTRACT
Largelanguagemodels(LLMs)havedemonstratedremarkablepotentialinsolv-
ingcomplextasksacrossdiversedomains,typicallybyemployingagenticwork-
flowsthatfollowdetailedinstructionsandoperationalsequences. However,con-

optimization of these workflows, but existing methods still rely on initial man-
